Implications

Emergency Mesh is ops-first. The next step is making the transport truly infrastructure-free across platforms.

What we have now

  • Web: real P2P mesh transport (WebRTC)
  • Minimal signaling service (WebSocket)
  • TTL forwarding for multi-hop delivery
  • Mobile: BLE phone-to-phone mesh transport
  • Mobile: Wi‑Fi Direct transport on Android

What closes the gap to Bitchat-like systems

  • Add E2EE + peer identity
  • Add iOS high-throughput local transport (Multipeer / Wi‑Fi Aware alternative)
  • Add multi-group bridging + better peer selection
